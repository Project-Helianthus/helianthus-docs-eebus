## Observed Attempt

An independent observer resolved the Helianthus SHIP service on the selected
Home Assistant host interface. With first trust closed, the TXT record exposed
`register=false`. During one authenticated five-minute window, the same service
exposed `register=true` while handshake auto-accept remained disabled. Closing
or expiring the window returned the local state to pairing closed.

No pairing candidate completed in this attempt and no persistent trust was
written. This evidence therefore supports the registration transition and its
separation from automatic acceptance only. It does not claim a trusted VR940
session, SPINE data, semantic support, or behavior outside this attempt.

The canonical redacted observer report digest is
`sha256:7c39868ae90fe7ac2a387224274f67aa609e7eb25d77aed1f56bf3a2687291b9`.
