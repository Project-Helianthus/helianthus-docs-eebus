# Vaillant VR940f / myVaillant eeBUS Target

## Purpose

VR940f/myVaillant is the first Helianthus eeBUS lab target. The first milestone
is raw visibility: sessions, services, topology, pairing state, and raw
evidence through read-only MCP.

## Non-Goals

- No GraphQL exposure.
- No Portal consumer workflow.
- No Home Assistant entities.
- No command routing.
- No promoted semantic fields.

## Evidence To Capture

- runtime status;
- local and remote identity with redaction;
- pairing state;
- sessions;
- visible and paired services;
- entity and feature graph;
- usecase claims;
- degraded states;
- restart and reconnect behavior.

## Prior Partial Observation

`EV-20260720-001` is a redacted partial/negative observation for protected
pairing. It observed the local first-trust registration transition with
automatic acceptance disabled, but did not observe a completed candidate,
SHIP session, OOB confirmation, durable trust write, or trusted reconnect. It
therefore establishes no remote reachability or observed runtime state and does
not complete the live gate below.

## Live Validation

The [canonical live validation gate](../evidence/ship-identity-live-validation.md)
binds the single local advertisement, protected expected-peer comparison,
transport ordering, callback provenance, and restart behavior into one
redacted run. The gate retains no private address, literal peer fingerprint,
packet capture, raw identity, or promoted semantic.

The identity and TXT behavior is normative derived design. Live validation is
pending, and this page does not claim deployed support.
